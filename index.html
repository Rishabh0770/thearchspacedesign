About
ArchSpace Design

ArchSpace design is research oriented structural and dynamic design firm, rooted in Solan, Himachal Pradesh. ArchSpace design is committed in building strong structures and achieving our goals through our expertise in designing and engineering.

ArchSpace Design is devoted in building dreams and creating spaces, with our proficiency in Project planning, designing, Project Visualization through 2D drawings and 3D Modelling, we make those designs physically feasible through our research and structural analysis. We monitor our projects daily to assure quality check and to ensure the construction is being executed as per the planning.

ArchSpace design emphasises structural strength, stability, durability and even load distribution, in order to construct structures that are hazard prone and safe.

ArchSpace design believe that building designs are not just sturdy structures - it is about building dreams through every space, wall and pathway to create new memories.
